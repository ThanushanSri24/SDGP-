About Us

We build tools that make every school commute trustworthy.

Navi Kid was sparked after our own frantic calls to drivers went unanswered. We blend live tracking technology, safety policy design, and compassionate UX so every pickup and drop-off is visible, timely, and accountable.

Today we coach local van crews, partner with schools, and co-design features with families to keep decisions human while the tech handles the heavy lifting.

The Problems We Solve

School transportation shouldn't be a source of stress and worry. These are the challenges parents face every day.

Safety Concerns

Parents don't know exactly where the van is or if it's being driven responsibly

Communication Gap

Constant calls between parents and drivers create frustration for both sides

Time Uncertainty

Vans are sometimes late with no explanation, creating stress and schedule conflicts

Emergency Response

No quick way to track or contact the van during accidents, traffic, or breakdowns
